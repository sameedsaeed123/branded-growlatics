HughesNet - America's #1 Choice for Satellite Internet

HughesNet is the nation's largest satellite internet provider, delivering high-speed internet access to millions of Americans, including those in rural and remote areas where traditional cable and DSL services are unavailable. With coverage in all 50 states, HughesNet ensures that no matter where you live, you can stay connected to what matters most.

Powered by advanced satellite technology, HughesNet provides reliable internet service with speeds up to 25 Mbps and generous data allowances. The service includes built-in WiFi, free standard installation, and a 24-month price guarantee, making it an excellent choice for rural homes, vacation properties, and businesses that need dependable connectivity regardless of location.
