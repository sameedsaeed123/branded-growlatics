EarthLink - America's Reliable Internet Provider

EarthLink has been a trusted name in internet service for over 30 years, providing reliable connectivity solutions to millions of Americans nationwide. As one of the nation's most established ISPs, EarthLink offers a variety of internet technologies including fiber, cable, DSL, and fixed wireless to ensure customers can find the right connection for their needs, regardless of location.

What sets EarthLink apart is its commitment to straightforward, customer-friendly service. With no data caps on most plans, transparent pricing, and award-winning customer support, EarthLink focuses on delivering a hassle-free internet experience. The company partners with leading network providers to offer fiber speeds up to 5 Gigs in select areas, while also maintaining robust DSL and cable options for areas where fiber isn't yet available.
